Eclectic Kitchen

Posted September 17, 2012March 10, 2018 justincox

You are looking at the sweet potato gnocchi and fried banana wontons from Eclectic Kitchen. This wasn’t our first stop at the Eclectic Kitchen truck, and it won’t be our last.

The gnocchi (small portion pictured, also available in large) was so great and the sweet potato added a great sweetness. It was a really great lunch. And the fried wontons was an extremely surprising portion size considering the price. And seriously, fried banana wontons covered in honey? Can you really go wrong?

You can find Eclectic Kitchen on Twitter.
